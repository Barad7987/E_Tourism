Gir National Park
Gir Forest National Park is a wildlife sanctuary in Gujarat, western India.
It was established to protect Asiatic lions, who frequent the fenced-off Devalia Safari Park,
along with leopards and antelopes. Gir Jungle Trail, outside the fenced area, traverses
deciduous forest and is home to wildlife including vultures and pythons.
Great White Rann of Kutch
The Great Rann of Kutch is a salt marsh in the Thar Desert in the Kutch District of Gujarat, India.
It is about 7500 km2 (2900 sq miles) in area and
is reputed to be one of the largest salt deserts in the world.
